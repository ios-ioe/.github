# Code of Conduct  
## Ignite Open Source (IOS)

### Our Pledge

We, as members, contributors, and leaders of **Ignite Open Source (IOS)**, pledge to make participation in our community a **harassment-free, inclusive, and welcoming experience for everyone**, regardless of background, skill level, or identity.

We are committed to fostering a community built on **respect, collaboration, learning, and open knowledge sharing**.

---

### Our Standards

We expect all community participants to follow these standards.

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful, kind, and constructive
- Welcoming newcomers and beginners
- Giving and receiving feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Encouraging learning and collaboration

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Trolling, spamming, or deliberately disruptive actions
- Public or private intimidation or threats
- Publishing others’ private information without permission
- Any behavior that creates an unsafe or hostile environment

---

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to **all IOS spaces**, including but not limited to:
- GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Community chats, forums, and social media platforms
- Workshops, hackathons, and in-person or virtual events
- Any interaction representing Ignite Open Source (IOS)

---

### Enforcement Responsibilities

Community maintainers, moderators, and organization members are responsible for:
- Clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking fair and appropriate corrective action
- Acting in good faith to protect the community

Moderators have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

### Enforcement Guidelines

Violations may result in actions such as:
- A private warning
- Temporary or permanent restriction from participation
- Removal of content or contributions
- Suspension or ban from IOS platforms

The severity of action will depend on the nature and frequency of the violation.

---

### Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it responsibly.

You can report issues by:
- Contacting the IOS maintainers privately
- Using GitHub’s reporting tools where applicable

All reports will be reviewed **confidentially**, fairly, and respectfully.

---

### Commitment to Fairness

We commit to:
- Handling reports without bias
- Protecting the privacy of reporters
- Ensuring fair and proportionate enforcement
- Prioritizing community safety and trust
